About This Project

Preserving the heritage of Telugu Christian hymns

Our Mission

Andhra Kraistava Keerthanalu is dedicated to preserving, documenting, and sharing the rich heritage of Telugu Christian hymns. Our mission is to make these beautiful expressions of faith accessible to everyone, ensuring that the spiritual and cultural significance of these hymns continues to inspire generations to come.

These hymns represent more than just songs‚Äîthey are a testament to the faith, devotion, and cultural identity of Telugu-speaking Christians. Each hymn tells a story, carries a message, and connects believers to their spiritual heritage.

Why This Matters

In an increasingly digital world, preserving cultural and spiritual heritage becomes both more challenging and more important. These hymns have been passed down through generations, often through oral tradition and handwritten collections. By digitizing and cataloging them, we ensure their preservation for future generations.

  • ‚ô™ Accessibility: Making hymns easily searchable and accessible worldwide
  • ‚ô´ Connection: Linking hymns with YouTube performances and multimedia
